ABOUT US

Making Dreams Come True

Kids Dream Haven is a charitable foundation dedicated to improving the lives of children and their families. Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where kids can thrive, dream, and achieve their full potential. We believe that every child deserves a chance at a better life, and we are committed to making that a reality through our various outreach programs and initiatives.

EMPOWER

Our mission is to create a haven for children where their dreams can become a reality. By offering support, care, and resources, we aim to empower children and their families, giving them the tools they need to succeed.


SPREAD JOY

We strive to put smiles on the faces of children and their families, especially during the festive seasons, by spreading joy and providing essential support.

BRING JOY

This December, our Christmas Outreach program, a special initiative designed to bring joy and relief to those in need during the festive season. Our goal is to reach and positively impact the lives of over 2000 children and their families.

Thank you for your support!

Together, we can create a brighter future for our children and spread joy during the festive season.

OUR GOAL



Kids Dream Haven:
Making Dreams
Come True

1. Feeding 2000 Kids:
We will organize a grand feast for 2000 children, providing them with nutritious meals and festive treats. This event aims to ensure that every child experiences the joy and warmth of the holiday season.

2. Orphanage Visit:
Our team will visit local orphanages to deliver provisions, foodstuffs, groceries, and financial support. We aim to ensure that these children feel the love and care they deserve, especially during Christmas.

3. Clearing hospital bills:
We will cover the hospital bills for 50 children whose parents are unable to afford the costs. This initiative aims to relieve the financial burden on these families and ensure that their children receive the necessary medical care.

4. Support for Widows:
In addition to supporting children, we will provide food and financial assistance to widows in our community. We aim to spread the spirit of giving and ensure that these women and their families have a joyous holiday season.
