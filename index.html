Our Mission

We strive to put smiles on the faces of children and their families, especially during the festive seasons, by spreading joy and providing essential support.

Feeding 2000 Kids:


This event aims to ensure that every child experiences the joy and warmth of the holiday season. We will organize a grand feast for 2000 children, providing them with nutritious meals and festive treats.

Clearing Hospital Bills


This initiative aims to relieve the financial burden on these families and ensure that their children receive the necessary medical care.

Support for Widow


In addition to supporting children, we will provide food and financial assistance to widows in our community. We aim to spread the spirit of giving and ensure that these women and their families have a joyous holiday season.
